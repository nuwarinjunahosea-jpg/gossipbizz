The Ugandan media and creative industry is mourning the sudden death of celebrated photographer Saad Shots, who passed away on Friday, 18 April 2025. Widely known for his captivating work in event and lifestyle photography, Saad was a prominent figure in the country’s visual storytelling scene. His passing has left many shocked, with heartfelt tributes pouring in from colleagues, influencers, and fans across the country.

The news of Saad’s death broke early Friday and quickly spread across social media platforms. Saad was a familiar presence at major public events, concerts, and social functions, where he consistently delivered striking imagery that captured the energy and emotion of each moment. The circumstances surrounding his death remain unclear at this time.

How did Saad Shots die on Good Friday?

Saad Shots died in a tragic motorcycle accident on Good Friday, 18 April 2025. Photos from the scene show his motorcycle badly damaged, with a broken rim and signs of a heavy impact, confirming the fatal crash. Just four days earlier, Saad had posted pictures of the same bike looking brand new, though it remains unclear whether he had just purchased it. He was riding it on Good Friday when the accident occurred, and he died on the spot. The fact that this happened on such a significant day has added an even heavier weight to the grief felt by those who knew and admired him.
