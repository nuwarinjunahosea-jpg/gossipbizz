FYNO UG INFO

According to Fyno, his catalog includes other popular tracks such as “Sabimanya,” “Waapi,” and “Bigatta,” among others. The singer also shared insights into how he has learned from well-established artists.

He acknowledged that studying their approach has helped him understand the formula for creating hit songs and maintaining a strong presence in the industry. It should be noted that Fyno UG is not new to the music industry. As a professional songwriter, he has been active for over a decade and has written hit songs for top Ugandan artists, including Nina Roz and Grenade, among others. His songwriting skills have played a key role in shaping his career and influencing the industry.
