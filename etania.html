Joshua Baraka marks upcoming anniversary with sweet surprise for Etania Gloria

Musician Joshua Baraka surprises Etania Mutoni ahead of their upcoming third anniversary as a couple. The couple will be celebrating three amazing years of love and happiness, and Joshua Baraka went all out to make the anniversary very special for his queen.

From Etania’s snap, we catch a glimpse of her walking into a room full of rose petals and lit with candles. Joshua Baraka then hands her a bouquet as a sign of their blossoming romance.

What made the gesture more romantic was that Joshua Baraka collected their close friends, who scribbled beautiful messages for the couple, and piled them up in a video.

While sharing the clip, Etania revealed that she couldn’t help but cry while watching it on screen. However the couple told the media about how they are'nt ready to hold a baby. Etania Forever told the media that "God hasn't prepared our journey for motherhood and fatherhood"
